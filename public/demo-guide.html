Section 1 — الموظف

Employee Experience

Walk through the daily attendance flow from an employee's perspective.

1

Open the app

Go to kunoz.vercel.app on your phone or desktop browser.

2

Log in as Employee

Use the test credentials:

3

View your dashboard

You'll see your attendance status for today, weekly stats, and upcoming shifts at a glance.

4

Clock In

Tap "My Attendance" in the sidebar, then tap "Clock In". Allow GPS location access and take a selfie photo to verify your identity.

Sidebar › My Attendance › Clock In
5

See confirmation

A green checkmark confirms your clock-in with your location name and timestamp.

6

Clock Out

When your shift is done, tap "Clock Out" from the same My Attendance page.

7

View history

Scroll down on the attendance page to see your full attendance records and working hours.

8

Sign out

Go to Profile (bottom nav on mobile) and tap "Sign Out" to switch to the admin account.

Section 3

Key Features to Notice

📱

Works on mobile

Add to home screen for a native app-like experience. No app store needed.

📍

GPS geofencing

Employees must be physically present at their assigned location to clock in.

📸

Photo verification

A selfie is required at clock-in to prevent buddy punching and confirm identity.

🔄

Offline mode

Works without internet. Records sync automatically when connection is restored.

📊

Excel export

Download any report as a .xlsx spreadsheet for payroll or compliance.

🔔

Real-time notifications

Alerts for late arrivals, missed clock-outs, and geofence violations appear instantly.

👥

Bulk check-in

Supervisors can clock in workers who do not have access to a personal phone.
